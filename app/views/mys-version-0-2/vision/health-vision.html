Health Programme

Health vision

The vision of the programme is that people with a health condition or disability will get the support they need.

In giving this support, we will provide a simple and responsive service and make timely, accurate, and understandable decisions that help people get the right support for them.

There are five categories or aims, and each category has four subcategories or examples.

Simple Responsive Timely Accurate Understandable
1 We will simplify processes and data capture so they are easier to complete 1 We will reduce waiting time in the service to speed up claims 1 Claimants will feel involved and informed throughout the journey 1 We will make decisions that are accurate to policy intent 1 Claimants will understand our communications first time and not need explanations
2 We will reuse information as much as possible and minimise the need for claimants to repeat themselves 2 We will tailor our journeys to claimant needs and circumstances 2 We will use digital channels where possible to minimise delays in exchanging information 2 We will implement data capture and storage that enables us to measure and assess the performance of the service 2 Claimants will understand what is expected of them at every stage of the journey
3 We will use what we know about claimants to make their journey easier 3 We will allow for flexibility in delivery rather than linked to existing business rules or processes 3 We will make the right decision first time to reduce the need to claimants to repeat the process 3 We will use reference and existing data to verify and confirm what we know 3 Claimants will know where they are in the process so reduce progress chasing
4 We will make consistent decisions 4 Claimants will trust that we have made the right decision and not challenge it
