Health Programme

Vision

The vision of the programme is that people with a health condition or disability will get the support they need.

In giving this support, we will provide a simple and responsive service and make timely, accurate, and understandable decisions that help people get the right support for them.

There are five categories or aims, and each category has four subcategories or examples.

Simple Responsive Timely Accurate Understandable
1 People will be able to find the right service for them 1 People will know what is happening as they progress through the service 1 We will gather the minimum information required to make a decision at the earliest point 1 We will gather the right amount of information to make sure our decisions are accurate 1 People will understand the decisions that have been made about them
2 People will only have to provide information once 2 All communications with (to or from) people will use the best available channel for them 2 Digital channels will be available 24/7 2 All communication with (to or from) people will be error free 2 All communication with (to or from) people will be easily understood
3 People will not need to learn how to use the service to get the right outcome from it 3 We will trust claimants to drive their own journey 3 People will get the right decision first time around 3 We will spend government money fairly and appropriately and ensure people get the money they are entitled to 3 People will feel we've listened to them and fully understood what they've told us
4 We will ensure there are no barriers to people using the service easily 4 We will tailor the service based on the things we know (or find out) about people 4 All information will be made available to those who need to see it, or update it, instantly 4 We will use data to make continuous improvements to the service 4 People will feel they have been treated fairly
