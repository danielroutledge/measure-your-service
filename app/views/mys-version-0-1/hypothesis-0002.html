Hypothesis 2

Removing physical assessment from phone

Supported

This hypothesis includes an action for a user that the team believes will have an outcome.

The hypothesis must be tested in some way, and may have risks.

We believe if we...

remove physical assessment options from phone interviews

for a...

Healthcare Professional

it will mean that...

they will not be prompted unnecessarily for info that isn't needed for this type of assessment.

We'll know we're right when...

they can capture sufficient information despite the removal of the 'physical' section.

However, this could be harmful if...

a physical assessment is needed but is not available in the interface.
