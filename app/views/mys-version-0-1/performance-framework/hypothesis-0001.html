Hypothesis 1

Capturing Assessment type

Supported

This hypothesis includes an action for a user that the team believes will have an outcome.

The hypothesis must be tested in some way, and may have risks.

We believe if we...

create a way to record whether the assessment was done by phone, video, in person, or on paper

for a...

Healthcare Professional

it will mean that...

they will only have to record the type of assessment once.

We'll know we're right when...

they successfully record in one place how they conduct an assessment.
