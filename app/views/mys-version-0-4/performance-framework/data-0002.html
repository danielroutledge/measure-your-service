Fields completed by paper assessment

Analysis of 'completed fields', i.e. those with any content in them, was inconclusive. All fields were completed at least some of the time.

We believe this is because some users are adding things such as 'Not Applicable' to fields they're not using.

We need to do deeper analysis around word count of content of fields.

Average word count by field

The word count of fields shows that some fields such as 'Observations' are being used infrequently and when they are used they have very little text.

As such our hypothesis is supported that some fields are not being used and can be removed. However, it's not that clear for other fields such as 'Social and Occupational history' so we may need to do further desk research or text analysis on fields to confirm that these fields are not being used.
