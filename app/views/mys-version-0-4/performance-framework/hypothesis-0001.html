Hypothesis 1

4 headings in justification box

Measuring

  • Detail
  • Data

Detail

During User Research, we have identified when HCP’s are handling PIP applications they are providing recommendations against each activity by copying and pasting a choice of 4 headings into the text box.

The HCPs are given four sentences to copy from a crib sheet and use as headers.

These 4 headers are:

  • Restrictions reported:
  • Recommendation and evidence used to support:
  • Reasons why the reported restrictions are not supported by evidence (where applicable):
  • Other descriptors supported by some evidence and the reasons why they haven’t been advised (where applicable):

This is being used as a guide as all HCP’s are new to completing PIP assessments.

The team want to find out:

  • Which of the 4 headings from the crib sheet are being used in justification for each descriptors?
  • Is the first header largely just repetition of the notes?
  • Do they use specific combinations? (eg if number 3 never number 4)
