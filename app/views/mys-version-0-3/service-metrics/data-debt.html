Why this should be measured

The department's strategic ambitions rely heavily on use of data and improving our data capability.

Our services should be designed and developed without creating problems for the future. Minimising data debt brings better opportunities to improve services and decision making.

Minimising data related debt

Debt, in a digital sense, is the cost of design or development approaches that aren’t strategic. These choices or priorities may give short term value but aren’t sustainable in the long term.

Debt should be a conscious choice, with a plan to repay as soon as possible to avoid paying too much interest.

Everyone should see data as a priority to support design and decision making at all stages.

By raising data as a priority your service will be better framed to measure success.
